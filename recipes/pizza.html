Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe is an easy and delicious way to make your favorite Italian dish at home. Customize with your favorite toppings!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices (or other toppings of choice)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough onto a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese and desired toppings like pepperoni.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Slice and serve hot!
