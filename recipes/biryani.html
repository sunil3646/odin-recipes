Briyani

Description

Briyani is a flavorful, aromatic rice dish made with spices, meat (usually chicken, mutton, or beef), and rice. It's a popular dish in many cultures, especially in South Asia.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, mutton, or beef
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice and soak for 30 minutes. Drain.
  2. Cook the meat with spices, onions, and tomatoes in a pot.
  3. In a separate pot, bring water to boil, add rice, and cook until 70% done. Drain.
  4. Layer cooked meat and rice, adding cilantro and yogurt in between layers.
  5. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with raita or a side salad.
