Chicken

Description

This chicken recipe is a simple yet flavorful dish, perfect for a quick meal. It can be served with rice or bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken pieces
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  3. Mix in chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add chicken pieces, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until the chicken is done.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.
