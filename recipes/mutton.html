Mutton

Description

This mutton recipe is a rich, flavorful dish that's perfect for special occasions. It combines tender mutton with aromatic spices for a mouth-watering result.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb mutton pieces
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a pot, heat oil and saut√© onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  4. Add mutton pieces, garam masala, and salt. Cook until browned.
  5. Stir in yogurt and cook until the mutton is tender and the gravy thickens.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.
